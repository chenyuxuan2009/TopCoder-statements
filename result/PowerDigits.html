Problem Statement for "PowerDigits"

Problem Statement

Given a positive integer a, consider the infinite sequence of integers {a^0, a^1, a^2, ...}. What proportion of them begin with the digits digits?

Definition

Class:
PowerDigits
Method:
proportion
Parameters:
int, int
Returns:
double
Method signature:
double proportion(int a, int digits)
(be sure your method is public)

Notes

  • The returned value must be accurate to within a relative or absolute value of 1E-9.

Constraints

  • a will be between 2 and 1000, inclusive.
  • digits will be between 1 and 999, inclusive.
