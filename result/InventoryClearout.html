Problem Statement for "InventoryClearout"

Problem Statement

To make room for newer products, a store would like to sell off its most unpopular items by discounting their prices and moving them to the clearance section. First, it must check its inventory records and determine which products have been selling poorly. Product information will be given as a String[], products, where each element represents a single product and is formatted as (quotes and brackets for clarity only): "<PRODUCT_ID> <PRODUCT_NAME>", where <PRODUCT_ID> is an integer which uniquely identifies a product, and <PRODUCT_NAME> is the name of the product. Inventory records will be given as a String[], inventory, where each element represents a single product and is formatted as (quotes and brackets for clarity only): "<PRODUCT_ID> <QTY_ORDERED> <QTY_IN_STOCK>", where <PRODUCT_ID> is an integer which corresponds directly to the <PRODUCT_ID>s in products, <QTY_ORDERED> is the number of units that have been ordered and received from the distributor, and <QTY_IN_STOCK> is the number of units in stock that have not yet been sold. Given an int, threshold, return the names of all the products that have sold at or below threshold percent of their ordered quantity. The product names should be returned as a String[] sorted in alphabetical order. If no products meet the criteria, return an empty String[].
