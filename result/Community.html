Problem Statement for "Community"

Problem Statement

Given an undirected graph, G, you want to find a subgraph G' of G such that the ratio (number of edges in G')/(number of nodes in G') is maximized. An edge is in G' if both of its endpoints are in G'. Return the maximum ratio.

The graph will be given as a String[] where character j of element i is '1' if and only if i and j are connected.

Method:

Parameters:

Returns:

Method signature:

(be sure your method is public)

Notes

  • G' must have at least one node in it for the ratio to be defined.

Constraints

  • g will contain between 2 and 50 elements, inclusive.
  • g will be square and each character will be '0' or '1'.
  • g will be symmetric and have no self-edges.
