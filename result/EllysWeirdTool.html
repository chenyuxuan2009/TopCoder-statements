Problem Statement for "EllysWeirdTool"

Problem Statement

Elly has a weird tool that can manipulate strings. It works as follows: The girl chooses two distinct indices of ÃÂ° string. The characters at these indices get deleted, and, in addition to that, the substring between them gets reversed.

For example let's assume the girl has the string "topcoder". She can use the tool on the indices 1 and 5 (indexed from zero) to obtain "topcoder" -> "tocper". After that she can use it on the 'o' and 'e' to obtain "tpcr". Finally, she can use it on the 'p' and 'c' to obtain "tr".

The girl initially has the String S. She wants to obtain the lexicographically largest possible string that can be derived from it by using the tool zero or more times. Help her by writing a method that returns the desired result.

Constraints

  • S will contain between 1 and 13 characters, inclusive.
  • Each character of S will be a lowercase letter from the English alphabet.
