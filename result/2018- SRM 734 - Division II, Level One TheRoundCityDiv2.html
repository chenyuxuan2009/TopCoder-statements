Problem Statement for "TheRoundCityDiv2"

Problem Statement

John and Brus have found a nice horizontal plane and they built a round city somewhere on the plane. The center of the city is at coordinates (0, 0). This point is left empty. There is a house at each other lattice point that is at most r units away from the city center. That is, the lattice point (x, y) other than (0, 0) contains a house if and only if sqrt(x*x + y*y) &le; r.

John and Brus now want to count all houses in the city. You are given the int r. Return the number of houses.

Definition

Class:
TheRoundCityDiv2
Method:
find
Parameters:
int

int

Constraints

  • r will be between 1 and 100, inclusive.
