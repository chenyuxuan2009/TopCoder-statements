Problem Statement

A useful statistic in many applications is the range of a group of numbers. The range is defined as the difference between the largest element in the group, and the smallest element in the group. For example, the range of {1,5,7,3,8} is 7, since the difference between 1 and 8 is 7.

Your task is to write a class Statistics, with a method getRange, which takes a int[], numbers, and returns the range of the elements of numbers.

int getRange(int[] numbers)
(be sure your method is public)

Constraints

  • Each element of numbers is between -1,000,000 and 1,000,000, inclusive.
