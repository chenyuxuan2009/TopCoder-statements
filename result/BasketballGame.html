Problem Statement for "BasketballGame"

Problem Statement

In this problem, you are given the log of successful throws during a basketball game. Your task will be to calculate the final score of the game. The throws are given in int[]s time, basket and points. The i-th throw is represented by the following parameters:
  • time[i] -- the time when the throw was made (in seconds since the beginning of the game).
  • basket[i] -- the ID of basket (1 or 2) into which the throw was made.
  • points[i] -- the number of points (1, 2 or 3) that this throw contributed to the score.
For simplicity, let's call the playing teams A and B. During the first half of the game (time[i] between 1 and 1440, inclusive) all throws into basket 1 increase the score of team A and all throws into basket 2 increase the score of team B. Once the first half of the game is over, the teams switch sides. Therefore, during the second half of the game (time[i] between 1441 and 2880, inclusive) all throws into basket 1 increase the score of team B and all throws into basket 2 increase the score of team A.

Let SA and SB be the final scores of teams A and B, respectively. Return a String of the form "SA:SB". Both SA and SB must be written without unnecessary leading zeros.

Definition

Class:
BasketballGame
Method:
getScore
Parameters:
int[], int[], int[]
Returns:
String
Method signature:
String getScore(int[] time, int[] basket, int[] points)
(be sure your method is public)

Constraints

  • time, basket and points will each contain between 1 and 50 elements, inclusive.
  • time, basket and points will contain the same number of elements.
  • Each element of time will be between 1 and 2880, inclusive.
  • Elements of time will be sorted in non-decreasing order.
  • Each element of basket will be between 1 and 2, inclusive.
  • Each element of points will be between 1 and 3, inclusive.

  1. {500, 1000, 1500, 2000}

    {1, 2, 1, 2}

    {1, 2, 3, 1}

    Returns: "2:5"

    At second 500, we have a successful throw into basket 1 for 1 point. The score is 1:0. At second 1000, we have a successful throw into basket 2 for 2 points. The score is 1:2. At second 1500, we have a successful throw into basket 1 for 3 points. As this throw is already in the second half of the game, the score is now 1:5. At second 2000, we have a successful throw into basket 2 for 1 point. The final score is 2:5.
