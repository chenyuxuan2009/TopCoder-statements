Problem Statement for "MultipleStops"

Problem Statement

A number of passangers are travelling in a bus along a road. Road is a straight line, and every passenger has its own destination, which is represented as distance in kilometers from the beginning of the road. The bus travels 1 kilometer per minute. The bus can stop at any point of the road, but the stop will take extra T minutes before the stop and T minutes after the stop. For example, if the bus starts at moment 0 and wants to make stops at points 5 and 10, it will stop at point 5 at the moment 5 + T (passengers will be able to leave at this moment), will start from the stop at the moment 5 + 2*T and will stop at point 10 at the moment 10 + 3 * T.

If a passenger left the bus not at his destination, he can reach his destination by legs. For every passenger it takes M minutes to pass 1 kilometer. Given numbers M and T, and a int[] destinations, representing the destinations for all passengers, you are to arrange bus stops to minimize the time when all passengers reach their destinations. Return this time.
