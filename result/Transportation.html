Problem Statement for "Transportation"

Problem Statement

You need to transport n packages numbered from 0 to n - 1. To transport packages you can rent a truck. Renting a truck to transport k packages costs a + k^2 dollars. The capacity of a truck is limited, so each truck can only transport packages with a total weight of at most c. You may need to rent a truck several times to transport all packages. Packages are ordered by their priority, so packages with smaller numbers must be transported no later than packages with larger numbers.

You are given a int[] w, the i-th element of which is the weight of the i-th package. Return the minimal total cost of transporting the packages.

Definition

Class:
Transportation
Method:
minCost
Parameters:
int[], int, int
Returns:
int
Method signature:
int minCost(int[] w, int c, int a)
(be sure your method is public)

Constraints

  • w will contain between 1 and 50 elements, inclusive.
  • c will be between 1 and 50,000, inclusive.
  • Each element of w will be between 1 and c, inclusive.
  • a will be between 1 and 1000, inclusive.
