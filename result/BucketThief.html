Problem Statement for "BucketThief"

Problem Statement

At the market, there are several buckets of fruit lined up. Feeling a bit mischievious, you notice that they are usually unattended. You want to take as many fruits as possible, without being noticed. However, you suspect that taking the fruit from two adjacent buckets would be too obvious.

You are given int[] fruit indicating the number of fruit in each bucket. Assuming that you will not take fruit from any two adjacent buckets, return the maximum number of fruit you can take.

Definition

Class:
BucketThief
Method:
maxTake
Parameters:
int[]
Returns:
int
Method signature:
int maxTake(int[] fruit)
(be sure your method is public)

Constraints

  • fruit will contain between 1 and 50 elements, inclusive.
  • Each element of fruit will be between 0 and 100, inclusive.

    Returns: 4

    There's no sense touching the middle bucket, as that would mean we can't take either of the other two. Best to take the 1 and the 3.

    Returns: 40

    The best we can do here is 18, 13 and 9.
